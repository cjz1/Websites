Tom's History

Tom's Overworld Dog Market has been in the business for around six months. While we don't have years of experience like other photography and pet sites, we have the commitment to one day become them. We were founded by a group of young web developers intending to create a website for entertainment. They have succeeded in their plan of entertainment, but now it is time for business, literally.

Our Mission

For our customers: Provide a product very few will be able to use, yet the ones who need them will have them easily available.
For our employees: Create a positive and safe working environment allowing them to create a change in the world.
For our owners: Spread goodness around.
For everyone: Provide fun, entertainment, and overall greatness.
